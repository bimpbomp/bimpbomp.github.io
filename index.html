Waves

This project is an attempt by me to create a 2D simulation of the sea (sortof). The plan is:

  • The player is in a boat
  • There are islands that the player cannot sail onto (obviously)
  • Different water depths and currents will force the player to think about where to move
  • Fish will be swimming around
